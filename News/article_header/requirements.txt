streamlit & pandas: Essential for the web interface and data table management.
newspaper3k: The core library for extracting article text and metadata.
lxml_html_clean & lxml==4.9.4: This is the "Compatibility Duo." Newer versions of lxml removed the HTML cleaner that newspaper3k relies on. Pinning version 4.9.4 is the industry-standard fix to keep your app from crashing.
nltk: Required for the .nlp() function to generate keywords and summaries.
